What's the world record for most tacos served in an hour?

Tacos Tacos, el taco chilango de la CDMX in Mexico City, Mexico, holds the Guinness World Record for most tacos eaten in an hour, with 8,450 tacos served in plates of 5 by 5 on February 24, 2024 (Source: Guiness book of records)
